Oatmeal Recipe

Description

This recipe is for a milk based bowl of oatmeal, with blueberries and almonds sprinkled on top. The milk can be replaced for any milk substitute, or water if desired.

Ingredients

  • 1/2c Rolled Oats
  • 1c 2% Milk
  • 1Tbsp Honey
  • Handful of Almonds
  • Handful of Blueberries

Steps

  1. Place pot on the stovetop and turn the heat to high
  2. Pour the cup of milk into the pot while it is heating and wait until it begins to boil
  3. Once boiling, pour oats into boiling milk, stir, and turn the burner to medium
  4. While the oats are heating, stir periodically when boiled milk begins to rise
  5. Once you have reached your desired consistency, turn the burner off and take the pot off the burner
  6. Allow the oatmeal to cool for 2 minutes, and then poor it into a bowl
  7. Next, take the honey and stir it in
  8. Finally, sprinkle the blueberries and almonds on top and enjoy
