Sushi

Description

This sushi recipe is a homemade version of a famously difficult dish to make. It will not be as luxurious as sushi made by a sushi chef, but it IS much cheaper.

One of the ingredients we will use in the recipe is raw fish, so handle this sensative ingredient with care.

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Avocado
  • Cream Cheese
  • Rice
  • Nori

Steps

  1. Pour 1/2c white rice (dry) into strainer
  2. Run water over rice in strainer until the water is no longer cloudy
  3. Place rice in rice cooker with 1c cold water
  4. Turn on rice cooker
  5. While rice is cooking, lay out the nori with the shy side towards the cutting surface (this will be the side we see once we roll it)
  6. Take your salmon and cut into thin, long strips (about the length of your longest finger)
  7. Wash your hands after handling raw fish
  8. Spread a layer of the rice on top of the nori, pressing gently without smashing
  9. Lay a row of each salmon, cream cheese, and avocado on top of the rice (enough to cover the shortest distance between two edges of nori)
  10. Roll up the sushi tightly using a bamboo mat
  11. Cut the pieces and enjoy
